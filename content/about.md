## Who We Are

We're a team building algorithmic trading systems for crypto markets. Our journey started with $86 in a Deribit account and a simple question: **Can a well-designed bot consistently beat the market?**

## The $86 Challenge

After losing 98.8% of our capital to flawed strategies (v1 through v22), we discovered the single most important factor in crypto perpetual futures trading: **maker vs taker fees**.

The same strategy that goes bankrupt paying 0.05% taker fees returns +233% with 0% maker fees. This insight transformed our approach.
